Making Associations in Memory

Instructions

In this experiment you will study N_STIMULI pairs of images. Each pair will consist of a unique image on the left (the "CUE") and a repeating image on the right (the "TARGET"). Your goal is to memorize each pair so that when given the CUE, you will be able to select the TARGET that goes with it.

You will be tested for your memory of the pairs at the end of the experiment. For each pair that you remember correctly, you will be paid a bonus of BONUS_PER_POINT_STR, on top of the BASE_PAYMENT_STR you receive for doing this experiment. If you're able to remember all the pairs correctly, you would earn a total of MAX_PAYMENT_STR.

    There are four parts to the experiment:
  1. Study the pairs (approx. 8 minutes)
  2. Decide which pairs you want to study again (approx. 8 minutes)
  3. Restudy some of the pairs (approx. 4 minutes)
  4. Test your memory (approx. 8 minutes)
