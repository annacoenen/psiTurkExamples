Part 3: Restudy phase

Instructions

In this part of the experiment you will get the opportunity to restudy some of the image pairs. As in the first study phase, you will see each pair for 4 seconds with the CUE on the left and the TARGET on the right. Try to create vivid mental images of the two objects interacting.

As before, between presentations of image pairs you will see an arrow point to the left or right. Press the corresponding arrow key in order to continue to the next pair.

When you're ready to begin, press Next.
